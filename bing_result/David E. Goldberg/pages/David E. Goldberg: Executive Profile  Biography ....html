Background

Dr. David E. Goldberg, also known as Dave, co-founded Nextumi, Inc., and served as its Chief Scientist. Dr. Goldberg is the Jerry S. Dobrovolny Distinguished Professor of entrepreneurial engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and Director of the Illinois Genetic Algorithms Laboratory. He applies his research in genetic algorithms to Nextumi's products and services. Prior to joining the University of Illinois, Dr. Goldberg taught at the University of ... Alabama, Tuscaloosa from 1984 to 1990. He held a number of positions at Stoner Associates of Carlisle, PA, including Project Engineer and Marketing Manager from 1976 to 1980. Dr. Goldberg serves as Member of Advisory Board of RiverGlass, Inc. He received the National Science Foundation Presidential Young Investigator Award in 1985 and is an associate of UIUC's Center for Advanced Study. Dr. Goldberg is founding chairman of the International Society for Genetic and Evolutionary Computation, and his book, Genetic Algorithms in Search, Optimization and Machine Learning, is one of the most widely cited references in computer science. He holds a BS in Civil Engineering (1975), MS in Civil Engineering (1976), and a PhD (1983) all from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.
