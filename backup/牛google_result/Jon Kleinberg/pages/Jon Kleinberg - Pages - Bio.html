Jon Kleinberg

TRANSWESTERN ROLE

As managing director of Transwesterns Investment Services group in Atlanta, Jon Kleinberg and his partners Kevin Markwordt and Mike McGaughy oversee all of the firms investment sales brokerage services in Atlanta and throughout the Southeast U.S. They are consistently ranked nationally among Transwesterns Top Investment Brokers.
 
Jon, Kevin and Mike offer a comprehensive and multi-faceted background successfully marketing and negotiating the sale of a wide variety of assets including multifamily, office, retail and industrial. They have completed transactions throughout the Southeast, the Mid-Atlantic, and Texas/ Southwest regions of the country.

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS

Jon has 26 years of commercial real estate experience in brokerage, dispositions, lending, and portfolio management. In his 18 years of investment sales brokerage, he has represented both private and institutional clients in the sale of investment properties valued in excess of $2.0 billion.
 
Prior to joining Transwestern, Jon spent ten years with Insignia/ESG in the firms Capital Advisors
Group, where he oversaw the firms investment sales services in Atlanta. He also spent seven years in the real estate department of the Travelers Insurance Company, where he concentrated on loan production, portfolio management, and asset disposition.

PROFESSIONAL AFFILIATIONS/RECOGNITION

Jon is a licensed real estate salesperson in the State of Georgia.

EDUCATION

Jon received his bachelors in economics and government from St. Lawrence University and his masters of business administration from Duke University.
