Jure Leskovec.

I am assistant professor of Computer Science at Stanford University. My research focuses on mining and modeling large social and information networks, their evolution, and diffusion of information and influence over them. Problems I investigate are motivated by large scale data, the Web and on-line media.

What's new

  • Teaching the Minning Massive Datasets MOOC on Coursera. Starts September 2015!

  • QUOTUS reveals the tructure of political media coverage as revealed by quoting patterns.
